TPS-Highlight

The only changes I will make to your texts are the ones described in this website: http://evidencescribe.com/es/tps/onlinemacro.aspx . Reposted here for your convenience:

What it does: this macro highlights all article adjectives at the start of the sentence and being verbs anywhere in red.

"That" and "got" are highlighted in yellow.

Apostrophes (') are highlighted in in orange. Remember, they are only bad if they are a contraction.

Words that start a sentence are in green. Words that have already been used to start a sentence are in red.
